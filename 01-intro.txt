# **Intro Functionality Proposal**

### Game Title: *World War Tanks*

## 1. Purpose of the Intro

The intro sets the tone of the game, explains the conflict quickly, and transitions the player smoothly into gameplay without long tutorials or text overload.

---

## 2. Intro Flow Overview

**Total duration:** ~10–15 seconds (skippable)

**Flow:**

1. Studio / Game Logo
2. Story Setup
3. Visual Tank Conflict
4. Call-to-Action (Start Game)

---

## 3. Intro Screens & Functionality

### 3.1 Logo Screen

**Functionality:**

* Display game logo: *World War Tanks*
* Simple fade-in animation
* Background: static battlefield silhouette
* Soft mechanical sound or engine hum

**Player Action:**

* Click anywhere to skip

---

### 3.2 Story Setup Screen

**Functionality:**

* Short text appears with typewriter or fade effect:

> “In a world where gravity is unstable, tanks no longer roll…
> They fly.
> And they fight for dominance.”

* Background slowly pans (parallax effect)

**Purpose:**

* Establish lore without long explanation
* Prepare player for antigravity mechanics

---

### 3.3 Conflict Visual Screen

**Functionality:**

* Two tanks appear on opposite sides
* One tank fires a shell in a curved arc
* Explosion animation on impact
* Screen shake (light)

**Purpose:**

* Visually teach:

  * Projectile arcs
  * Turn-based combat idea
* No UI yet — pure visual storytelling

---

### 3.4 Start Game Screen

**Functionality:**

* Buttons:

  * **Start Battle**
  * **Controls** (optional)
  * **Mute Sound**
* Subtle tank idle animation
* Background music loop starts here

**Player Action:**

* Click **Start Battle** → Load Level 1

---

## 4. User Controls

* **Mouse / Touch:**

  * Click anywhere to skip intro
  * Button-based navigation on final screen

---

## 5. Technical Considerations (Phaser)

* Intro implemented as a **Phaser Scene**
* Assets preloaded before intro starts
* Intro scenes chained using timers or scene transitions
* Skip functionality available at all times

---

## 6. Success Criteria

* Player understands:

  * Tanks shoot projectiles
  * Physics-based gameplay
* No confusion before gameplay
* Fast transition to action

---

## 7. Future Expansion (Optional)

* Animated comic panels
* Dynamic text based on difficulty
* Multiplayer intro variations
